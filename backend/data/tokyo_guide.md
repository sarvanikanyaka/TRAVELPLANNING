# Tokyo Guidebook

## Lodging options
- Budget: Capsule Hotel Shinjuku. Located in Shinjuku district. Simple capsules, free Wi-Fi, clean shared showers. Price: 40 USD per night. Rating: 4.1 stars.
- Mid-Range: Hotel Sunroute Plaza Shinjuku. Near Shinjuku station. Comfortable rooms, great location, buffet breakfast. Price: 130 USD per night. Rating: 4.4 stars.
- Luxury: Park Hyatt Tokyo. Premium high-rise hotel in Nishi-Shinjuku. Luxury spa, indoor pool, stunning city view. Price: 550 USD per night. Rating: 4.8 stars.

## Sightseeing and Activities
- Sensō-ji Temple: Historic Buddhist temple in Asakusa. Built in 645 AD. Recommended time: Morning. Duration: 2 hours. Cost: Free. Category: Historic.
- Shibuya Crossing: Iconic pedestrian crossing. View the neon signs, visit Starbucks for overhead photos. Recommended time: Evening. Duration: 1 hour. Cost: Free. Category: Shopping.
- teamLab Planets: Interactive digital art museum in Toyosu. Walk through water, sensory rooms. Recommended time: Afternoon. Duration: 3 hours. Cost: 25 USD. Category: Museum.

## Dining Options
- Ichiran Ramen: Specializes in tonkotsu ramen. Solitary eating booths. Location: Shibuya. Average cost: 15 USD. Cuisine: Japanese.
- Gyukatsu Motomura: Famous for deep-fried beef cutlets cooked on stone grills. Location: Shinjuku. Average cost: 22 USD. Cuisine: Japanese.
