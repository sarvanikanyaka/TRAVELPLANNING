# India Guidebook (Mumbai, Goa, Delhi)

## Lodging options
- Budget: Zostel Hostel. Budget backpacker hostel. Social lounges, clean bunk beds, gaming area. Price: 800 INR per night. Rating: 4.2 stars.
- Mid-Range: Bloom Boutique. Comfortable modern rooms, signature yellow decor, great breakfast, centrally located. Price: 4000 INR per night. Rating: 4.5 stars.
- Luxury: The Taj Palace. Iconic premium palace hotel. Sea views, world-class butler service, high-end fine dining, wellness spa. Price: 22000 INR per night. Rating: 4.9 stars.

## Sightseeing and Activities
- Gateway of India: Historic arch monument by the Arabian Sea. Watch the yachts, take photos, feed pigeons. Recommended time: Morning. Duration: 1 hour. Cost: 0 INR (Free). Category: Historic.
- Elephanta Caves: Ancient rock-cut cave temples on Elephanta Island. Accessible by ferry from Gateway of India. Recommended time: Afternoon. Duration: 4 hours. Cost: 250 INR. Category: Historic.
- Colaba Causeway Market: Vibrant street market selling jewelry, handicrafts, and clothes. Recommended time: Evening. Duration: 2 hours. Cost: 0 INR (Free). Category: Shopping.

## Dining Options
- Leopold Cafe: Historic restaurant and bar. Famous for multi-cuisine dining, beer towers, and lively vibe. Location: Colaba. Average cost: 700 INR. Cuisine: Indian.
- Britannia & Co: Legendary Parsi restaurant famous for Berry Pulav, Sali Boti, and caramel custard. Location: Ballard Estate. Average cost: 1000 INR. Cuisine: Parsi.
