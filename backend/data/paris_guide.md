# Paris Guidebook

## Lodging options
- Budget: Generator Paris. Modern designer hostel in the 10th arrondissement. Clean rooms, rooftop bar, social atmosphere. Price: 50 USD per night. Rating: 4.0 stars.
- Mid-Range: Hotel Caron de Beaumarchais. Charming boutique hotel in Marais district. 18th-century style, French breakfast. Price: 160 USD per night. Rating: 4.5 stars.
- Luxury: Le Bristol Paris. Historic palace hotel near Champs-Elysees. Michelin-starred dining, rooftop pool, luxury spa. Price: 850 USD per night. Rating: 4.9 stars.

## Sightseeing and Activities
- Eiffel Tower: Iconic iron tower. Climb or take the lift to the top deck for panoramic views. Recommended time: Afternoon. Duration: 2 hours. Cost: 28 USD. Category: Historic.
- Louvre Museum: The world's largest art museum. See the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and glass pyramid. Recommended time: Morning. Duration: 3 hours. Cost: 22 USD. Category: Museum.
- Seine River Cruise: Relaxing boat cruise passing Notre Dame, Louvre, and Eiffel Tower. Recommended time: Evening. Duration: 1 hour. Cost: 18 USD. Category: Historic.

## Dining Options
- Angelina Paris: Famous historic tea room known for thick hot chocolate and Mont-Blanc pastry. Location: Rue de Rivoli. Average cost: 25 USD. Cuisine: French.
- Le Relais de l'Entrecôte: Famous for steak frites with secret green herb sauce. Location: Montparnasse. Average cost: 42 USD. Cuisine: French.
