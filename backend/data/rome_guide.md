# Rome Guidebook

## Lodging options
- Budget: The Beehive. Cozy eco-conscious hostel near Termini Station. Quiet garden, cafe, organic breakfast. Price: 45 USD per night. Rating: 4.2 stars.
- Mid-Range: Hotel Santa Maria. Charming hotel in a former 16th-century convent in Trastevere. Courtyard, free bike rentals. Price: 140 USD per night. Rating: 4.6 stars.
- Luxury: Hotel de Russie. Premium luxury hotel near Piazza del Popolo. Terraced gardens, fine dining, luxury spa. Price: 700 USD per night. Rating: 4.8 stars.

## Sightseeing and Activities
- Colosseum: Ancient Roman amphitheater. Recommended time: Morning. Duration: 2 hours. Cost: 18 USD. Category: Historic.
- Trevi Fountain: Stunning Baroque fountain. Toss a coin to ensure your return to Rome. Recommended time: Evening. Duration: 0.5 hours. Cost: Free. Category: Historic.
- Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel: Massive collection of art, Michelangelo's ceiling frescoes. Recommended time: Afternoon. Duration: 3.5 hours. Cost: 25 USD. Category: Museum.

## Dining Options
- Da Enzo al 29: Tiny trattoria famous for Roman pasta dishes like carbonara and cacio e pepe. Location: Trastevere. Average cost: 20 USD. Cuisine: Italian.
- Frigidarium: Famous gelateria offering artisanal gelato dipped in dark or white chocolate. Location: Piazza Navona. Average cost: 5 USD. Cuisine: Italian.
